## üÜò Common Questions

**Q: Why can't I see all the opportunities?**
A: Check your filters. Click "Show All" or adjust the stage filter.

**Q: Where did my completed tasks go?**
A: They're in the "COMPLETED" section. Click "Clear completed tasks" to remove them.

**Q: The weather shows the wrong location**
A: This happens when location services are disabled. The app uses your IP address as a fallback. Enable location services for more accurate weather.

**Q: Can I undo marking a task as complete?**
A: Yes! Just click the checkbox again to uncheck it.

**Q: How do I add a note to an account?**
A: Click the account, scroll to the "Notes" section, and click "Add Note".

**Q: Can the AI assistant make mistakes?**
A: The AI is very helpful but always review its suggestions before saving data. You have final approval.

**Q: How do I convert a lead to an opportunity?**
A: Open the lead, click "Convert to Opportunity", confirm the details, and it will create an account, contact, and opportunity.
